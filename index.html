About Me
The person behind the code.

I'm a pre-final year AI & Data Science student at MBM University, Jodhpur who genuinely enjoys the process of building — not just training models, but designing the systems around them.

I got into AI because I wanted to understand how machines can read, reason, and respond like humans do. Most of my projects are fully offline and production-aware because I believe good AI engineering means thinking about the whole system, not just the model.
